Table: retail_table

Columns:
- InvoiceNo (text)
- StockCode (text)
- Description (text)
- Quantity (integer)
- InvoiceDate (timestamp)
- UnitPrice (numeric)
- CustomerID (integer)
- Country (text)

Business Rules:
- Revenue = Quantity * UnitPrice
- One InvoiceNo can contain multiple rows
- Each row represents one product in an invoice
- Exclude returns:
    Quantity > 0
